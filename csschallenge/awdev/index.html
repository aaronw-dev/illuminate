Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What even is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a competition where programmers and graphic designers come together to create a project from scratch. During a hackathon, there will also be other fun activities and educational workshops. At the end of the hackathon, teams will present their project, and there will be prizes!

Q: Who can participate?

Anybody can participate!
